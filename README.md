📌 Overview

This project presents a comprehensive E-commerce Sales Dashboard designed to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions such as revenue, customer behavior, product trends, and delivery efficiency.

The dashboard provides actionable insights that help in understanding customer purchasing patterns, identifying top-performing products, and optimizing operational efficiency.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze overall sales performance

Understand customer buying behavior

Identify top-performing sales channels

Evaluate product popularity by gender

Monitor delivery performance and customer satisfaction

📊 Key Metrics

Total Orders: 2,400

Total Quantity Sold: 11,997

Total Revenue: ₹6,49,020

Average Rating: 4.0 ⭐

Average Delivery Time: 2.3 Days

📈 Dashboard Features
🔹 Sales Trend Analysis

Weekly performance tracking (last 13 weeks)

Revenue and quantity trends visualization

🔹 Customer Purchase Behavior

Distribution of how many items customers buy

Identification of bulk buyers

🔹 Sales Channel Contribution

App, Website, Instagram, Partner Apps comparison

Channel-wise performance insights

🔹 Geographic Analysis

Customer distribution by location

Region-wise sales concentration

🔹 Delivery Performance

Shipping time analysis

Efficiency tracking

🔹 Customer Satisfaction

Rating distribution (1 to 5 stars)

🔹 Product Analysis

Product popularity by gender

Category-wise performance insights

💡 Key Insights

📱 Mobile App contributes ~40% of total sales, making it the top-performing channel

🛒 Majority of customers prefer bulk purchasing (10+ items)

👕 Categories like Jeans and T-Shirts show high demand

🚚 Average delivery time is efficient (~2.3 days)

⭐ Customer ratings are generally positive (around 4.0 average)
